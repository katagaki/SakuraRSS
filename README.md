# Sakura Reader

RSS reader that offers multiple ways to read, discover, and enjoy your content.

Integrations (may break at any time but checks are put in place to keep it running):
- YouTube player
  - Embedded WebView that fits to the video frame
  - Tries to minimize distractions
  - Does not block ads
- X feeds
  - Links out to X app (this will remain for now)
- Instagram profiles
  - Links out to the Instagram app (this will also remain for now)
